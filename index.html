My Favourite Meal

The name of my favourite food is Bread and Beans

About my favourite food

Bread is a continental food but I am not sure if Beans is continental.

List of Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity/Measurement
Bread One Loaf
Beans Two cups
Onions Two big bulbs
Pepper Half of small plate
Palmoil Three spoons

Preparation method

  1. Select the Beans
  2. Wash the Beans
  3. Perboil the Beans
  4. Grate the two bulbs of Onions
  5. Put the Beans on fire and add the grated Onions. Leave it to simmer for a while
  6. Add a measured Quantity of salt when the Beans is soft and let it simmer for another few minutes
  7. Then add a blended pepper and palmoil without stirring the Beans and let it simmer for a while
  8. Stir the Beans while it is on fire and let it simmer for just a while before finally putting off the fire underneath the pot of Beans
